About:

The Recipedia App allows you to Search for Recipes and Save the Recipes

How it works:

  1. Search for your recipes by entering the name into the search bar.
  2. Select a recipe within the results, this will take you to the recipe page for your selection.
  3. Save the recipe by clicking on the icon in the top Navbar. This will save the recipe to My-Recipe Page.

Removing a Recipe

If you no longer need to save the recipe, simply select recipe within My recipe page. At the navbar, click the icon in the top Navbar
